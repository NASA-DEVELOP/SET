The Team

The Skyglow Estimation Toolbox (SET) is a community project funded under NASA DEVELOP. Researchers need better methods for quantifying the impact that anthropogenic light sources have on night skies, to better understand human and animal helath as well as to protect one of the most beautiful features of our universe.

SET is designed to do just that. We hope that the National Park Service and other public lands managers make use of this tool to determine how their night skies are changing over time and where and which anthropogenic light source are haning the most impact.
